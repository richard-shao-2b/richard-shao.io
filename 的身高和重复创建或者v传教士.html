The history of pepper; as early as 5000 years ago, the Mayans ate pepper on the American continent. But since the Ming Dynasty's merchant ships brought pepper to America, the Chinese have used pepper in hot pot.According to historical records, the earliest time to eat pepper in Guizhou and Hunan was during the Qianlong period of the Qing Dynasty, and the most common time was after Daoguang. Later, it was widely cultivated in all parts of China. It was the most widely used spice in China

Capsicum annuum L. is an annual or Limited perennial herb of Magnolia, Solanaceae and capsicum. Stem subglabrous or puberulent, branches slightly zigzag. The leaves are alternate, the apical nodes of the branches are not elongated, forming twins or clusters, oblong ovate, ovate or ovate lanceolate, entire, apex short acuminate or acute, base narrowly cuneate; flowers are solitary, drooping; calyx cup-shaped, not significantly 5-toothed; corolla white, lobes ovate; anthers gray purple. The stem is thick and drooping; the fruit is long finger shaped, the top is gradually pointed and often curved, green when immature, red, orange or purplish red when mature, and spicy. Seeds oblate reniform, pale yellow.
