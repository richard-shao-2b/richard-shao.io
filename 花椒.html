Zanthoxylum bungeanum Maxim. Is a small deciduous tree belonging to Rutaceae and Zanthoxylum bungeanum Maxim. The spines on the stems up to 7 meters high often fall early, with short spines on the branches, leaflets on the leaves and narrow wings on the leaf axis. The leaflets are opposite, sessile, ovate, elliptic and thin lanceolate. The spines on the top of the leaf axis are larger, and the leaf edge has cleft teeth and oil spots on the teeth. The other flowers have no or scattered oil spots, the midrib is slightly depressed on the leaf surface, the inflorescence is terminal or on the top of lateral branches, the inflorescence axis and pedicel are densely pubescent or glabrous; the perianth segments are yellow green, with the same shape and size; the female flowers rarely develop stamens, carpels, and the style is obliquely bent back. The fruit is purplish red, with a single petal with slightly raised oil spots and a very short awn tip at the top. It blooms from April to May and bears fruit from August to September or October. China is distributed from north to south of northeast, South to North Slope of Wuling, southeast to Jiangsu and Zhejiang
